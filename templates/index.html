Get Familiar With Causal AI

Focuses on understanding and learning cause and effect relationships within data. Causal AI uncovers the 'why' behind AI, like when AI predicts a stock price increase, Causal AI examines the factors influencing this increase—why it’s happening. This enables users to analyze crucial factors that generative AI overlooks. Explainable AI strives for transparency in AI decision-making via predictive models, but it doesn't clarify the influencing factors or the 'why' behind predictions, whereas, Causal AI does.
